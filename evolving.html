Brand

Mous is inspired by the rich history of underground hip hop culture in New York City. This is a genre that represents creativity and authenticity, and the Mous brand is committed to preserving that spirit through and with our community and project. We welcome everyone join us on the 44th Street Subway Station - The train will be arriving shortly.

Experimenting

Experimentation is key to unlocking the full potential of communities, projects and technology. Pushing boundaries, exploring new creative directions and trying new things is what we feel is necessary. If you are looking for a place to explore your creativity, trying new things or looking for like-minded people, we are the place for that.

Community

Community is the heart of our brand. A vibrant community of enthusiasts and artists who share a love of the underground vibes, community, art and web3. Collaboration and support are essential to the creative process, and we are proud to build a community that allows and promotes that. If you are looking for creativity, diversity and inclusivity, we are here.

44 Labs

We are proud of our our start-up development company 44 Labs. Building through our company allows us to innovate and bring excellence to our community. We are able to leverage cutting-edge technology and expertise to create value and revenue for our project through own use and sales. Come to our underground lab and let's push technological boundaries together.
